Welcome

Here at Asset And Property Research Professionals we provide non legal homeownership advice to property owners that wish to either sell, renovate or lease their property. Our well trained data research teams' spend countless hours a day either helping homeowners make the right decisions for themselves on the status of their home;or our staff is researching data to see who is owed unclaimed funds from a city, a state, a government entity or a private business.Then we locate them.

Yes, we know our client's privacy is of the utmost importance which is why we go the extra length to protect their privacy so don't be worried.We do not pass on any personal information that we come across nor do we store customers personal data after their service is completed with us regardless of whether or not their information is already public record.Protecting your privacy is our number one mission.

Although we are not operating as licensed real estate brokers, real estate agents or real estate attorneys;we do have qualified individuals that understand marketing, advertising and assigning contracts. We work with commercial property owners, residential property owners, property owners about to lose their homes and former property owners who lost their home and need some counseling.Any problems a homeowner wishes to fix inregards to property management, asset protection or property research our professionals can help.

The staff at Assets And Property Research Professionals pride ourselves on helping individuals prevent foreclosure if possible, secure unclaimed funds whether from an old employer who didn't correctly follow a W-2 form and left funds on the books under their name, or an old bank accout that you closed years ago but not before a direct deposit from a old tax refund went through that you never claimed. We are a multifaceted data analyzing company that serves the general public.

Today, many companies routinely take advantage of the elderly, the disabled along with the non-english speaker but not us.Our team at Asset And Property Research Professionals know how it feels to have a hospital billing company overcharge them or have an employer who left funds on the books for them they haven't collected yet.

We are here to serve the interest of our clients no matter how complex or difficult the situation may be. Companies like ours step in to help the uninformed, the non-english speaker, and the elderly who may not fully understand the sometimes complex issues in regards to their home.
